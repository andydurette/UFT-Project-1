Is My Neighbourhood Safe?

We all consider the safety of ourselves and our loved ones, wheather just to be aware of potential dangers or for consider where you want to live and work. To put minds at ease we have built a tool to answer these questions, below you can use the provided tools to compare Toronto's crime stastics by district, neighbourhood and even see crime by neighbourhood year to year to see if it's increasing or decreasing.

A special thanks to the Toronto police whose open data has made this possible.

Know your city and its dangers

Each marker on the map refers to the different police divisions and the neighbourhoods that they cover. Click on the division and view all the reported crimes from 2014 to 2018.

Compare crimes between neighbourhoods

Chose two police divisions and a neighbourhood in each division to compare the all reported crimes from 2014 to 2018.

How has my neighbourhood been doing?

Compare yearly data in a neighbourhood to see if the crime is getting better or worse.
